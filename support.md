## Frequently Asked Questions

**How do I cancel my subscription?**
Subscriptions are managed directly through Apple or Google, not by us:
iPhone: Settings → your name → Subscriptions. Android: Play Store →
Profile → Payments & subscriptions. You'll also find a direct link in the
app under Profile → Manage subscription.

**How do I delete my account?**
Directly in the app: Profile → Delete account. This removes your data from
the cloud and from the device.

**How do I export my data?**
Profile → Export my data — you'll get a JSON file with all your entries.
